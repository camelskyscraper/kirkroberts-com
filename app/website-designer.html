Hi, my name is Kirk.

My main gig is doing custom website development for fancy design firms that (rightfully) charge a lot of money.

But.

I also enjoy collaborating directly with individuals who need a “website designer” but have a more modest budget.

Our goals for your site are likely to be...

  • organization
  • a sense of purpose
  • clear communication
  • appropriate and consistent tone
  • ease of use (for site visitors)
  • a “clean” and professional look
  • ease of use (for site editors to update the site)

Depending on your needs we can collaborate on...

  • purpose definition
  • site structure (sections, pages)
  • text content oversight
  • image curation
  • page layout
  • selecting and customizing a visual theme
  • choosing and linking third-party services (social media, email lists, video hosting, etc)
  • setting up e-commerce
  • mobile considerations
  • domain (e.g. your-company.com) signup and linking
  • email service selection and linking

I highly, highly recommend we use Squarespace to build and host your site. They provide lots of great tools and attractive starting templates we can customize to our needs. There are definitely limitations, but overall I feel that the trade-offs are worth it. In many cases this is true even if you’re already invested in another service.

You might even just want to have a go at it yourself. Really, give it a try! There is a free trial.
